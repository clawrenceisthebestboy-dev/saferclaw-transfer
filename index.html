SaferClaw Transfer
Free One-Time Clone Tool

Clone your entire OpenClaw setup from one Mac to another in minutes. No account needed. No license key. Just works.

⬇ Download for Mac — Free
macOS 14+ · Apple Silicon · ~1 MB
1

Backup

Run SaferClaw Transfer on your old Mac. Click "Backup This Mac" — it packages everything into one .tar.gz file on your Desktop.

→
2

AirDrop

AirDrop the backup file to your new Mac. Takes about 30 seconds. No cloud upload needed.

→
3

Restore

Open SaferClaw Transfer on your new Mac. Click "Restore From Backup" — it reinstalls everything and gets OpenClaw running.
