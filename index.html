🦞 SaferClaw
Transfer Edition — Free

Clone Your OpenClaw Setup
to a New Mac in Minutes

Everything transfers — configs, projects, memory, bots. Backup on old Mac, AirDrop, restore on new Mac. Done.

1

Run the Installer

Download and double-click the installer. It sets up Homebrew & Node automatically.

SaferClaw-Installer.command
2

Install the App

Download SaferClaw Transfer, open the DMG, drag to Applications.

SaferClaw-Transfer.dmg
3

Backup Old Mac

Open the app on your old Mac and click Backup This Mac.

4

AirDrop to New Mac

Right-click the backup file → Share → AirDrop → select your new Mac.

5

Restore

On your new Mac, open the app, click Restore From Backup, select the file.
